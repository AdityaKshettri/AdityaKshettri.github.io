Biography


Hello guys! this is Aditya. I am a Full Stack Web Developer, specialized in Backend Development using Java Spring Boot and Frontend Development using JavaScript (mainly React.js/ Angular.js). Currently, I am working as a Software Engineer in Societe Generale Global Solutions Centre, Bangalore. My domain, being Full Stack Web Development, I develop REST APIs for Asset and Liability Management for the bank.

Talking about my education, I have done my High School and Intermediate from Little Flower School, Gorakhpur. I have done my Undergraduation from Vellore Institute Of Technology, Chennai Campus in the domain of Electronics and Computer Engineering. I have done Internship at Signals and Communication Department, North Eastern Railway, Gorakhpur, UP. regarding smoothening the training program for all the trainees, who have cleared Railway exams, using a Time Table Management System which was designed and developed by me.

Since I studied both Electronics and Computer Science together in my B.Tech, I am in relentless pursuit of knowledge about how we can leverage the power of both the fields and come up with many efficient devices for the safety of people at very low costs. Even though tremendous progress has been achieved in this field, but surprisingly, there are very few gadgets which are affordable by the common people. That is the main reason why women are still not safe in this country. Hence, it has been my dream all along to design affordable gadgets for human safety. For this, I have worked on so many projects regarding human safety, the most recognized one among those being my dream project, Smart Safety Jacket with Safe Route Assistance, which I even got patented. This way, I will be working on many more such projects in future.

Projects


Smart Safety Jacket with Safe Route Assistance Website

  • In this project, we have designed a Smart Jacket for the sole purpose of safety of its user using automatic accident detection, alert system and sending user location to hospitals and police stations in case of any emergency.
  • We have also designed a Safe Route Assistance web app which will calculate the safest route from a given source to a given destination for the user based on the danger index calculated using previous criminal records on each route.

VAAK - Voice Augmented Assisstive Kit Website

  • VAAK is a college project under the TARP course, where we had to make groups and come up with creative solutions to real world problems and implement them.
  • We made a project that helped specially-able people who cannot speak by making a device that took input from their fingers in a fixed format (ex: American Sign Language), process it and play out the processed data via a speaker that is either interfaced with an android smartphone or simply a smartphone's inbuilt speaker.
